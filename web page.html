SERVICE

A car service is when you take your car to a garage for a routine check up. A mechanic will look at your car’s condition and check parts for wear and tear, like brakes, oil, filters and engine belt. It’s common for the garage to replace your oil filter as part of the service. They may recommend that you replace other car parts too.
